**Distribution:**
- Press release via PR Newswire

**Promotion Strategy:**

**Real results build credibility**

## 🎯 HARO (Help A Reporter Out)

### Strategy
1. **Sign up**: https://www.helpareporter.com/
2. **Subscribe**: IT, Technology, Business topics
3. **Respond quickly**: Within 1-2 hours
4. **Provide value**: Be the expert source

### Response Template
```
Subject: RE: [Query Topic] - IT Expert Available

Hi [Reporter Name],

I'm [Your Name], [Title] at BloomTechUSA, with [X] years of experience in [relevant field].

Regarding your query on [topic]:

[Direct, concise answer to their question]

[Supporting data or example if relevant]

[Additional insight that adds value]

I'm happy to provide additional information or quotes. You can reach me at:
Email: [email]
Phone: (737) 329-8158

Background:
BloomTechUSA is a premier technology solutions provider based in Austin, TX, specializing in [relevant services].

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## ⚠️ What to Avoid

### Bad Link Building Practices
❌ Buying links
❌ Link farms/PBNs
❌ Automated link building
❌ Irrelevant directory spam
❌ Comment spam
❌ Article spinning
❌ Low-quality guest posts

### Red Flags
- Sites offering "instant" backlinks
- "Build 1000 links for $50"
- Link exchanges with unrelated sites
- Hidden links
- Exact match anchor text overuse

---

## ✅ Quality Over Quantity

### What Makes a Good Backlink?

✓ **Relevance**: From IT/tech industry sites
✓ **Authority**: High domain authority (DA 40+)
✓ **Traffic**: Site gets real visitors
✓ **Editorial**: Naturally placed, not paid
✓ **Dofollow**: Passes link equity (when possible)
✓ **Context**: Surrounded by relevant content
