## 🚀 Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Create Your Profile

1. **Go to Google Business Profile**
   - Visit: https://www.google.com/business/
   - Click "Manage now"
   - Sign in with Google account (use business email)

2. **Enter Business Name**
   - Type: "BloomTechUSA"
   - If it appears in the list, claim it
   - If not, click "Add your business to Google"

3. **Choose Business Category**
   - **Primary Category**: IT Services & Computer Repair
   - **Additional Categories**:
     - Software Company
     - Computer Consultant
     - Business Consulting Agency
     - Artificial Intelligence Company

4. **Add Location**
   - ✅ Yes, I serve customers at my business address
   - Enter address: 11801 Domain Blvd 3rd floor, Austin, TX 78758
   - Adjust map pin if needed for accuracy

5. **Add Service Areas** (Optional)
   - Austin, TX (primary)
   - Texas (statewide)
   - United States (nationwide)

6. **Add Contact Information**
   - **Phone**: +1 737 329 8158
   - **Website**: https://bloomtechusa.com

7. **Verify Your Business**
   - Choose verification method (usually postcard or phone)
   - **Postcard**: Sent to business address (5-14 days)
   - **Phone**: Instant verification call
   - **Email**: If available for your category

### Step 6: Gather Reviews

#### Request Reviews Strategy

1. **After Project Completion**
   - Email template with review link
   - Thank you message with request
   - Make it easy (direct link)

2. **Review Link**
   - Get your review link from Google Business Profile
   - Format: https://g.page/r/[YOUR_ID]/review
   - Add to email signatures
   - Include on invoices

3. **Email Template**
   ```
   Subject: We Value Your Feedback - BloomTechUSA
   
   Hi [Client Name],
   
   Thank you for choosing BloomTechUSA for your [project name]. We're committed to delivering exceptional service and would love to hear about your experience.
   
   Would you mind taking 2 minutes to share your feedback?
   
   [Leave a Review] → [Your Review Link]
   
   Your insights help us improve and assist other businesses in making informed decisions.
   
   Thank you for your time!
   
   Best regards,
   The BloomTechUSA Team
   (737) 329-8158
   ```

4. **Respond to ALL Reviews**
   - Thank positive reviewers
   - Address negative feedback professionally
   - Show engagement and care
